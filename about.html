A short introduction to me

Hello, I'm Vivanne! I'm a second-year student at the Hogeschool van Amsterdam, pursuing a degree in Communication and Multimedia Design (CMD). This program has been instrumental in improving my skills as a frontend developer and UX designer. A minimalist style has been nurtured in my designs, evolving with each project undertaken. Blending artistic elements into simple layouts presents an enjoyable challenge for me. Projects, in my view, should be well-organized and structured, and I have a keen eye for specific skills I aim to develop.

Beyond my studies, a passion for reading and quality time with two wonderful friends in our Amsterdam apartment fills my days. Cozy nights in with them are something I always look forward to. Leiden is where I grew up, but the allure of experiencing life in the big city as a student led me to move, and now I couldn't imagine it any other way.
